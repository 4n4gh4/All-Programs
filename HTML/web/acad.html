Insight on ABP's academics


  1. 1 0 th G R A D E
    1. Experience
      ABP was super excited in the beginning because Corona had begun and all classes were held online. But that led to a not so fun experience at home.
    2. Exams
      Dishonesty became prevalent everywhere and ABP was never in the mood to learn anything. Ended up struggling for everything at the last moment because ABP had been busy watching a TV show (a Turkish one-Dolunay).
    3. Conclusion
      The year sucked. But ABP wasn't aware of what was yet to come.
  2. 1 1 th / 1 2 th G R A D E
    1. Experience
      Hell hell hell. ABP began failing subjects, became the class clown, stuck around with the kind kids who also failed most of the time. Added to everything there was NEET coaching for a kid who could only dream of literature. Those two years were the most dynamic, dark and careless years in the life of ABP.
    2. Exams
      ABP was the last in class many times. Mathematics was the major villain most of the time and even the teachers began making fun of ABP.
    3. Conclusion
      ABP wanted to get away from everyone and everything back home including ex-classmates, ex-teachers, parents... everyone.
  3. C O L L E G E
    1. Experience
      ABP managed to escape everyone she hated and surprisingly overcame most (not all) demons that haunted her and began having fun again. Forever ABP will be grateful to the people that made her laugh.
    2. Exams
      ABP began working absurdly hard because her fire was fueled by all the hurtful things she had to hear. So mainly ABP is in her redemption arc now :]
    3. Conclusion
      ABP is gonna obliterate everything and everyone that looked down on her. Periodt.
